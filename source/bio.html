About

Florian Eckerstorfer was born and raised in Linz, Austria. After finishing HLW für Kommunikations- und Mediendesign he moved to Vienna to study Software & Information Engineering at Vienna University of Technology. He received his bachelor degree in July 2011. In October 2011 he began studying Software Engineering & Internet Computing and the supplement curriculum Innovation in March 2012.

Florian began working on internet related projects at a very early age. During school he worked at media and advertising agencies during the summer and freelanced for some years. After moving to Vienna he started working for 2beFOUND as Web Developer. He later transitioned to its new sister company 2bePUBLISHED to develop websites and web applications using state-of-the-art technology.

Besides school, university and work Florian always tries to invest as much time as possible into his very own projects. Since 2006 he writes a weblog, since 2007 it is called Web Adventures. You can view the full list of Florians side projects on the projects page.
